My football team

Since 1992, we have won the Premier League 13 times. In total, we have won a record 20 English League titles, a record 12 FA Cups, 4 League Cups, 3 European Champions Cups and 1 FIFA Club World Cup, making us one of the most successful clubs in England.

From being a little girl my Grandad would always buy me and my sibling the latest shirt, I loved attending games and learning all about the history of the club. Still to this day my passion for the team runs deep.
